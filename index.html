Mutare

"Nestled in the far eastern edge of Zimbabwe, right along the border with Mozambique, Mutare is the country’s third-largest city and serves as the scenic provincial capital of Manicaland. It is beautifully cradled within a natural bowl-shaped valley surrounded by the striking Christmas Pass hills. Because of its strategic border location, Mutare acts as a vital economic gateway to the Indian Ocean port of Beira. The city beautifully blends a relaxed atmosphere with rich cultural heritage, vibrant timber and agricultural industries, and close proximity to stunning tourist gems like the nearby Vumba Mountains and Nyanga National Park."
