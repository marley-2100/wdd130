    History

    For over three decades, Victoria Falls Rafting company has been a trusted leader in delivering world-class river adventures on the Zambezi. As a family-owned and operated business, we have built our reputation on a foundation of safety, passion, and an intimate knowledge of these legendary waters. Our journey began with a commitment to sharing the majesty of the falls with explorers from around the globe, and that dedication remains at the heart of everything we do today. With a team of highly experienced, expert guides, we continue to provide unforgettable, adrenaline-filled experiences while ensuring the highest standards of care for every guest who joins us on the river.
